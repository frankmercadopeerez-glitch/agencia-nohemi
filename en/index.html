The Nohemi Difference

More than an agency: your trusted companion in Cartagena

Nohemi and her team don't just plan your trip, they experience it with you. With every experience you get Nohemi's direct, personal companionship and the agency's security backing: she herself shows you where to shop and how to exchange your money at a fair local rate, and takes you to the best places while protecting you from the number one danger for tourists in Cartagena: currency-exchange scams and theft. That's what makes us different.

Safe Currency Exchange

We take you to the right places to exchange your money at a fair rate, with no surprises, no markups, and no risk.

Protection from Risk #1

Currency-exchange theft is the most common danger for tourists in Cartagena. We stay by your side so it never happens to you.

Trusted Local Guide
