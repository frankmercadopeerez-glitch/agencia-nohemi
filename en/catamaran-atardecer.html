Frequently Asked Questions

What drinks are included?

Unlimited drinks throughout the cruise: whisky on the rocks, Cuba Libre, mojito, margarita, beer or michelada, tequila sunrise, michelada sodas, soft drinks, juice and water.

What's the difference between Silver and Gold?

Silver Membership ($100,000 COP / person) includes unlimited open bar, live DJ, bartender, entertainer and VIP transfer to 2 nightclubs. Gold Membership ($180,000 COP / person) includes everything in Silver plus 3 food tasting courses: tropical fruit salad, Caribbean mini bowl and Spanish tasting.

Where is the meeting point and what are the times?

Meeting point is Muelle de los Pegasos in Cartagena. Available times: 5:00–7:00 PM, 7:00–9:00 PM and 9:00–11:00 PM. Spots are limited, so we recommend booking in advance.

Is it good for a bachelor or bachelorette party?

It's one of the most requested experiences for bachelor/bachelorette parties, birthdays and group celebrations. Tell us about the occasion on WhatsApp and we'll coordinate all the details for a perfect night.
