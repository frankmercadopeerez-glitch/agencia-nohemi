Cartagena City Food Tour

Walk, taste and discover the real Cartagena through its street food.

Home / Experiences / Cartagena City Food Tour
Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

Beyond its walls, balconies and sunsets, Cartagena is experienced with all your senses through its street food. The Cartagena City Food Tour takes you walking through the Old Town and the vibrant Getsemaní neighborhood, tasting the typical bites that locals eat every day.

Nohemi and her team accompany you at every stop, choosing trusted vendors, sharing the story behind each dish, and always guaranteeing a fair price, no surprises.

What's included?

Guided walking tour Tastings included History and local culture Small or private groups

Tip: come hungry and wear comfortable walking shoes. The tour combines several short stops, so it's best to pace your appetite across all the bites.

Perfect for

This tour is perfect for those who want to go beyond conventional tourism: families, couples and groups of friends who want to experience Cartagena through its culture and its people. It's also available as a private experience for your group.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Cartagena City Food Tour?

It includes a guided walking tour through the Old Town and Getsemaní, food tastings at each stop, and history and local culture shared by Nohemi or her team, always guaranteeing fair prices with no surprises.

How much do the food tastings cost?

Reference prices per bite are: arepa de huevo $4,000 COP, butifarra $3,000 COP, carimañola $3,500 COP, tropical fruit $3,000 COP, natural juice $5,000 COP and traditional sweet $2,500 COP.

Is the tour on foot or by boat?

It's a fully walking tour through the streets of the Old Town and the Getsemaní neighborhood, perfect for experiencing Cartagena's culture and cuisine up close.

Can I do the tour as a private group?

Yes, the tour is available both for small groups and as a private experience for your family or group of friends. Message us on WhatsApp to coordinate the date and time.
