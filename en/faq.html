We've Got Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your experience with us in Cartagena de Indias.

Bookings and Payments

How do I book an experience or boat rental?

You can book directly from our Fleet or Experiences pages by adding your plan to the cart and completing the booking form, or by messaging us on WhatsApp at +57 316 303 0589. Nohemi and her team will confirm availability and the final details with you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept online card payments through our payment gateway (Mercado Pago), and we can also arrange cash or transfer payments on the day of the tour. For international bookings, we recommend paying online or coordinating in advance via WhatsApp.

Are prices in Colombian pesos or US dollars?

All prices are calculated in Colombian pesos (COP). For your convenience, you can use the COP/USD button at the top of the site to see an approximate conversion to US dollars.

Do I need to pay 100% in advance?

It depends on the plan. For many experiences we request a deposit to confirm the booking, with the balance paid on the day of the tour. We'll let you know the exact terms when confirming your reservation.

Cancellations and Changes

Can I cancel or change the date of my booking?

Yes. We understand that travel plans change. Message us on WhatsApp as far in advance as possible and we'll do our best to reschedule your experience, subject to availability. Check the specific terms for each plan in our Terms and Conditions.

What happens if the weather doesn't cooperate on the day of the tour?

Safety comes first. If sea or weather conditions are unsafe, we'll offer to reschedule at no extra cost or adjust the itinerary. A cloudy sunset, however, is not grounds for cancellation — they're beautiful in Cartagena too!

Is there a refund if I cancel at the last minute?

Policies vary depending on the type of service (private boat, group experience, kitesurfing, etc.). Check the relevant section in our Terms and Conditions, or ask us directly before booking to avoid surprises.

Before Your Experience

What should I bring?

We recommend biodegradable sunscreen, sunglasses, swimwear, a change of dry clothes, a towel, and cash for personal expenses (such as the entrance fee to the Corales del Rosario National Natural Park, if applicable).

Is there a minimum age for activities?

Most of our boat trips are suitable for the whole family, including young children under adult supervision. For activities like kitesurfing or diving, we recommend checking the specific minimum age when booking.

Do you include transportation from my hotel?

For several experiences we can arrange the most convenient meeting point for you, including pickup from hotels in the historic center, Bocagrande or Manga. Ask us when booking to confirm if your accommodation is within our coverage area.

What about food and drinks on board?

Some experiences include snacks, ice and drinks; others are customizable. Let us know your preferences (including dietary restrictions) when booking.

Safety and Certifications

Do the boats have official permits?

Yes. We operate registered vessels with the corresponding permits from the maritime authority (DIMAR), and we carry personal accident insurance for our passengers.

Are there life jackets on board?

Yes, all of our boats have life jackets for every passenger, which are mandatory during navigation when indicated by the crew.

What happens if I have a medical emergency during the tour?

Our crews are trained in basic first aid and maintain constant communication with land. For more details on coverage and responsibilities, see our Terms and Conditions.

Groups, Languages and Contact

How many people fit on each boat?

Capacity varies by vessel, from intimate boats for small groups to yachts for large groups and events. Visit our Fleet page to see the capacity of each option.

Do you organize private events or celebrations?

Yes! Birthdays, bachelor/bachelorette parties, anniversaries and corporate celebrations are part of our specialty. Tell us the occasion and we'll put together a custom plan.

Do you speak English?

Yes, our team can communicate in Spanish and English to assist international travelers before, during and after the experience.

What's the best way to contact you?

WhatsApp is our fastest channel: +57 316 303 0589. You can also message us on Instagram or Facebook (@dunasyolas_ctg / @dunasyolas).
