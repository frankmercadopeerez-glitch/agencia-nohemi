Barú · Puerto Medallo Beach Club

Playa Blanca, Barú Day Trip

Beach club experience with round-trip transport, welcome cocktail, lunch and pool access facing the Caribbean.

Home / Experiences / Playa Blanca, Barú
Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

Playa Blanca on the island of Barú is one of the most beautiful beaches in Colombia — crystal-clear water, white sand and the laid-back pace of the Caribbean coast. Our day trip takes you there by land (no rough water crossings), directly to the Puerto Medallo beach club, where everything is already set up for a perfect day.

Forget planning — transport, food, pool and a beach lounger are all included. Just bring your swimsuit, sunscreen and your best Caribbean mood.

What's Included?

  • ✅ Round-trip land transport from Cartagena.
  • ✅ Welcome cocktail on arrival. 🍹
  • ✅ Lunch with your choice of: chicken, pork or fish.
  • ✅ Access to the balcony and sun loungers facing the sea.
  • ✅ Pool access (subject to availability).
  • ✅ Freshwater shower (one per person).

Pickup Points & Times

Pickup time: 7:00 AM – 8:00 AM

We pick you up at: Bocagrande, Laguito, Torre del Reloj, ARA Marbella, CAI Crespo or Contecar. We confirm your exact point and time when you reserve.

Perfect For

Families Couples Beach lovers First-time visitors

Tip: this land-transport option is much more comfortable than the boat version of Playa Blanca — no seasickness, air-conditioned vehicle, and you arrive directly at the beach club with everything ready. Outside food and drinks are not allowed at the venue.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Playa Blanca Barú day trip?

Includes round-trip land transport, welcome cocktail, lunch (chicken, pork or fish), access to facilities — balcony, sun loungers and pool — and a freshwater shower per person.

What are the pickup points and times?

Pickup is between 7:00 AM and 8:00 AM in Bocagrande, Laguito, Torre del Reloj, ARA Marbella, CAI Crespo or Contecar. We confirm your exact point and time when you reserve.

Can I bring my own food or drinks?

No. Outside food and drinks are not allowed at the venue. Lunch and the welcome cocktail are already included in your day pass.

How is this different from going to Playa Blanca by boat?

This trip uses comfortable land transport, avoiding the open-water crossing. The Puerto Medallo beach club offers a more organised and comfortable experience than the public section of Playa Blanca, with facilities, food service and a pool included.
