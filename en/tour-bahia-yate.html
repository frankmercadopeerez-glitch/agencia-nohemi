Sail, celebrate and create unforgettable moments with open bar, DJ and entertainment on board.

Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

Experience Cartagena Bay the right way: aboard the Luna Mía yacht. Cruise between the lights of the walled city and the Bocagrande skyline while the party takes over. ⚓

With an open bar on selected drinks, DJ on board, entertainer and bartender, this tour is the perfect plan to celebrate, toast and dance on the water. Just show up — we handle the rest. ✨

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Luna Mía Yacht Bay Tour?

Includes the bay cruise with open bar on selected drinks, live DJ, entertainer, bartender and a unique atmosphere on Cartagena Bay.

What are the departure times?

3 daily departures: 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM, 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM and 9:00 PM – 11:00 PM. Each cruise lasts approximately 2 hours.

Where does the yacht depart from?

The departure point is Muelle de los Pegasos, Cartagena de Indias, next to the Historic Old Town. We'll confirm the exact boarding point when you reserve.

Do children pay full price?

Children up to 12 years old pay 50% of the per-person fare.
