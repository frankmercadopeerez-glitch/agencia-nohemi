Cartagena Nightlife · Party Bus + Club

Chiva Rumbera Cartagena

Crossover music, bilingual entertainment and VIP entry to Taboo nightclub aboard Cartagena's iconic party bus.

Home / Experiences / Chiva Rumbera
Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

Experience Cartagena's best night out aboard a Chiva Rumbera — the colorful traditional bus that transforms into a rolling dance floor, touring the city's most iconic landmarks while the crossover music never stops. 🪩🕺

Immerse yourself in the magic of Cartagena with a tour full of fun, bilingual entertainment and, to close the night in style, VIP entry to Taboo nightclub included. The perfect plan for groups, bachelor/bachelorette parties and anyone who wants to experience the most authentic nightlife in the city.

What's Included?

  • ✅ Pickup and drop-off at the meeting point.
  • ✅ Stop at the Zapatos Viejos (Old Shoes) monument. 👟
  • ✅ Panoramic stop at San Felipe Castle. 🏰
  • ✅ Stop at the Cartagena Letters landmark. 🔤
  • ✅ Crossover music with bilingual entertainment. 🎶
  • ✅ VIP entry to Taboo nightclub. 🪩

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Chiva Rumbera?

Includes pickup and drop-off at the meeting point, a stop at the Zapatos Viejos monument, a panoramic stop at San Felipe Castle, a stop at the Cartagena Letters, crossover music with bilingual entertainment and VIP entry to Taboo nightclub.

What are the departure times?

There are two shifts: Shift 1 from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM and Shift 2 from 9:00 PM to 11:00 PM. Each shift has staggered pickups in Marbella/Crespo, Bocagrande and Centro & Getsemaní.

Where will they pick me up?

Pickup is arranged in three zones: Marbella/Crespo (Hotel Corales de Indias parking, Ara Marbella, Cartagena Letters), Bocagrande (on Av. San Martín: Banco Itaú, McDonald's, Burger King, Juan Valdez, La Fontana, C.C. Nao, Parque Flanagan) and Centro & Getsemaní (Convention Centre fountain).

Why is the 9:00 PM shift from Centro shorter?

For the 9:00 PM shift with pickup from Centro Histórico, the tour lasts approximately 1 hour and 20 minutes due to current municipal decree restrictions.
