Your kitesurf spot in the Caribbean

Why Kitesurf with Dunas & Olas

The beaches of northern Cartagena (La Boquilla) are one of the best kitesurf spots in the Caribbean: steady wind almost all year round, warm and shallow waters, ideal both for your very first kite pull and for riders looking to perfect jumps and transitions. We coordinate certified instructors, well-maintained gear, and transport to the spot, so all you have to worry about is enjoying the Caribbean wind.

Certified instructors

Direct support from Nohemi and the agency's safety standards in every lesson and rental.

Wind all year round

La Boquilla offers great conditions for beginners and advanced riders most of the year.

Quality gear

Kites, boards, harnesses and wetsuits in good condition, checked before every session.
