Magical Sunsets in Cartagena Bay

Sail for 3 hours facing Bocagrande, the Old Town and Castillogrande while the sky turns orange and pink over the Caribbean.

Home / Experiences / Magical Sunsets (Bay Tour)
Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

Cartagena de Indias saves one of its best shows for the end of the day: a sunset that paints the bay, the walled Old Town, Bocagrande and Castillogrande in gold and violet. Our Bay Tour takes you sailing through these waters for 3 hours, right when the Caribbean light turns most beautiful and the city switches on its first lights.

It's one of the most requested plans for first-time visitors to Cartagena, couples looking for a special moment, and families or groups of friends who want to end the day with a view you simply can't get from land.

What's included?

  • Approximately 3-hour cruise across Cartagena Bay.
  • Privileged sunset views of Bocagrande, the Old Town, Manga and Castillogrande.
  • Life jacket and all on-board safety measures.
  • Direct accompaniment by Nohemi or her team throughout the tour.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the Bay Tour and what time does it depart?

The tour lasts approximately 3 hours, with departures between 6:00 PM and 12:00 AM depending on availability and sunset time. Nohemi and her team will help you choose the best time to watch the sun set over the bay.

What's included in the $120,000 COP per person price?

It includes your spot on the shared tour across Cartagena Bay, a life jacket, and direct accompaniment by Nohemi or her team, with the agency's safety backup throughout the tour.

Can I rent the whole boat for my group?

Yes. If you prefer full privacy, you can rent the entire boat starting at $3,200,000 COP (Interceptor 38', 16 pax) up to $3,900,000 COP (Virginia 420, 28 pax), great for groups, families or special events.

Is it a good option for a marriage proposal or romantic dinner?

Yes, it's one of the favorite plans for marriage proposals, romantic dinners, birthdays and bachelor/bachelorette parties. Tell us about the occasion and we'll coordinate the details to make it unforgettable.
