Rosario Islands Day Trip (Sunny Day)

A full day sailing among islands, mangroves and turquoise-water beaches just off Cartagena.

Home / Experiences / Rosario Islands Day Trip (Sunny Day)
Direct accompaniment by Nohemi, backed by the agency's safety standards

The Corales del Rosario y San Bernardo National Natural Park is one of the most important natural treasures of the Colombian Caribbean: an archipelago of islands, cays and coral reefs surrounded by turquoise waters less than an hour by boat from Cartagena.

Our Sunny Day trip takes you through this paradise for a full day, with stops to swim, sunbathe and enjoy some of the most photographed destinations in the country. It's the perfect plan for anyone who wants to experience Cartagena beyond its streets and walls.

What's included?

  • Full-day tour through the Corales del Rosario y San Bernardo National Natural Park.
  • Sailing on boats fully equipped for each destination.
  • Life jacket and all on-board safety measures.
  • Direct accompaniment by Nohemi or her team throughout the day.

Available destinations

Depending on the day's itinerary and sea conditions, you may visit:

Rosario Islands Baru – Cholon Baru – Playa Blanca Tierra Bomba Punta Arena Bocachica San Bernardo Archipelago Isla Fuerte

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Rosario Islands day trip?

It includes your spot on the shared tour through the Corales del Rosario y San Bernardo National Natural Park, a life jacket, and direct accompaniment by Nohemi or her team, with the agency's safety backup throughout the day.

What are the departure and return times?

Departure is on request from 8:00 AM, and the return to the dock is approximately at 5:00 PM, giving you plenty of time to enjoy several destinations during the day.

Can I rent the whole boat for my group?

Yes. If you prefer full privacy, you can rent the entire boat starting at $3,200,000 COP (Interceptor 38', 16 pax) up to $3,900,000 COP (Virginia 420, 28 pax), great for groups, families or special events.

Which destinations can I visit during the day trip?

Depending on the day's itinerary you can visit the Rosario Islands, Baru (Cholon and Playa Blanca), Tierra Bomba, Punta Arena, Bocachica, the San Bernardo Archipelago and Isla Fuerte.
